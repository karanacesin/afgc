What We Do

OUR CHURCH: Altoona Full Gospel welcomes the presence and power of the Holy Spirit, believing the manifestation of all of the New Testament gifts for the edifying of His people. We strive for clear and accurate Bible teaching, Spirit led praise and worship, active intercession ministry, and commitment to world outreach. By faith we accept the finished work of the Lord Jesus Christ on the cross, and desire that He be the center of our lives and our fellowship.

OUR YOUTH GROUP: Our desire is to challenge teens to grow in their knowledge of the Word, pursue an intimate relationship with Jesus Christ, and recognize the power of the Holy Spirit in their lives.

OUR CHILDREN'S MINISTRY: Your child will learn to love the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, while "fishing for truth" in His Word. They may act out Bible stories, pray and play with their classmates, and "hide God's Word in their hearts".
